## Scope
Risk assessment and prophylaxis to prevent hospital-associated venous thromboembolism.

## Risk Assessment
Assess every adult admission within 14 hours using the network VTE risk tool, balancing thrombosis risk against bleeding risk.

## Prophylaxis
Offer low molecular weight heparin to at-risk patients unless contraindicated. Use mechanical prophylaxis when bleeding risk is high.

## Renal Adjustment
Reduce low molecular weight heparin dose in significant kidney impairment; detection follows the Acute Kidney Injury Pathway.

## Restart After Bleeding
When anticoagulation is held for bleeding, restart timing is coordinated with the Anticoagulation and Bleeding Risk Pathway.
