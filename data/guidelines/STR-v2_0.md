## Scope
Adds mechanical thrombectomy and extended time windows with advanced imaging.

## Recognition
FAST retained. Pre-hospital large vessel occlusion screening triggers direct transfer to a thrombectomy centre.

## Imaging
Non-contrast CT plus CT angiography to identify large vessel occlusion. CT perfusion guides extended-window selection.

## Thrombolysis
Alteplase within 4.5 hours; tenecteplase is an accepted alternative. Thrombectomy is offered up to 24 hours in selected patients with favourable perfusion imaging.

## Anticoagulation Interaction
For patients on direct oral anticoagulants, follow the Anticoagulation and Bleeding Risk Pathway for reversal agents before any intervention.
