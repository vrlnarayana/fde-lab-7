## Scope
Prevention, detection, and management of delirium in hospitalised older adults.

## Detection
Screen at-risk patients with the 4AT tool on admission and when behaviour changes.

## Common Triggers
Common precipitants include infection, dehydration, constipation, pain, and medications. Sepsis and acute kidney injury are frequent medical triggers.

## Management
Treat the underlying cause first. Use non-drug measures: reorientation, sleep, hydration, mobility. Reserve low-dose antipsychotics for severe distress.

## Medication Review
Review and minimise deliriogenic drugs. For patients with infection, ensure antibiotic doses are correct for kidney function via the Antimicrobial Stewardship Pathway.
